- Each +2 cost ≈ ×4 slower (doubly-exponential scaling)

## Notes

- **Throughput** = 1 000 ms ÷ avg_time (higher = faster).
- **Peak RSS delta** = RSS increase above baseline during the call.
- Password length has very little impact on hash time — **cost factor dominates completely**.
- cost=14 takes **~2 seconds** per hash — recommended only for long-term archive storage where
  offline brute-force resistance is critical.
- The high variance in timing (min/max spread) reflects OS scheduling jitter, not algorithmic noise.
